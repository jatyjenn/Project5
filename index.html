About Us

At Scooba Steve's, our sole mission is to provide quality diver education. Our courses offer both the flexibility and convenience of doing your knowledge development online while in the comfort of your own home. Our Instructors are available to answer your questions while progressing through the online course. We're a simple email away.

Diving towards a healthier planet.

Throughout its deep history, Scooba Steve's has demonstrated a longstanding commitment to environmental conservation through its course offerings and alignment with organizations, such as the Project Loreum Foundation. Scooba Steve's conservation focused courses are designed to foster a sustainability mindset in every diver, helping to increase the number of ocean stewards around the globe.
