About Us

At Scooba Steve's, our sole mission is to provide quality diver education. Our courses offer both the flexibility and convenience of doing your knowledge development online while in the comfort of your own home. Our Instructors are available to answer your questions while progressing through the online course. We're a simple email away. By offering online knowledge development, we reduce the time spent in a traditional classroom allowing us to focus on your pool skills.
